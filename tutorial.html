How to study during Global Pandemic

I decided to do a case study with a few students to give a better variety of ideas that work for some and might work for you. It is important to stay connected with friends and family to avoid the feeling of being isolated.

FYI:DONT FORGET TO SCROLL DOWN UNTIL END

First student

How do you study during global pandemic?

The nature of online studying means that I can be a bit more flexible with my timetable. I have learned to work during the hours where I am most productive. I have two planners- one for weekly and the other for daily tasks.

The weekly one is useful because it allows me to see the bigger picture, whilst the daily one allows me to see each individual task in detail. At the start of the week I work on these plans and focus on the closest deadlines.

I have found studying at home pretty difficult as I always like to study in cafes and libraries. To overcome this, I have found studying in different areas of the house to be useful as it switches things up a bit.

Also, cooking breaks and lots of coffee!!!

How do you avoid distraction?

I find it really hard to avoid distractions but I find setting fake deadlines puts more pressure on myself which makes me more productive. For example, setting an essay deadline one week before the real deadline.

Another thing that helps is having someone working on something in the same room as you- if they’re focussed you’ll more likely to focus too.

(I think)

How do you keep yourself motived during this difficult time?

I think of my next career step constantly and listen to people who I find inspirational in the work that they do!!! I want to use my degree and experience to hopefully help people in the future so every time I’m struggling I think of that.

Also I always keep in mind that this situation is temporary !!

😀

Thank you 😀

Second student

How do you study during global pandemic?

I use the pomodoro technique with some music via youtube.

How do you avoid distraction?

By taking small breaks in between my studies.

How do you keep yourself motived during this difficult time?

Talking with my family and friends to keep myself going and thinking about my goal. Exercise to keep the mind occupied and getting enough sleep.

Thank you 😀

Third Student

How do you study during global pandemic?

I like to plan what i have to do in advance and thats helps me to organise my time wisely and reduce stress. Also, i like to take break after 1 hour or so.

How do you avoid distraction?

I often put my phone on silence when i am studying to avoid any possible distractions.

How do you keep yourself motived during this difficult time?

I think of my goal(e.g to graduate with a good grade) and try to stay focus on achieving it. It is important to have a purpose on anything you do.

Thank you 😀

Fourth student

How do you study during global pandemic?

How do you avoid distraction?

Keep phone away, make sure you are not hungry too, so you do not end up cooking dinner instead.

How do you keep yourself motived during this difficult time?

It is going to end eventually and looking back i dont want to think i wasted all this time.
